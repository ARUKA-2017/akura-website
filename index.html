What is AKURA?

An Automated Knowledge Understanding and Recognition Assistant (AKURA) is a generic platform developed to facilitate and streamline the build process applications which focuses on Content Scraping, NLU and Ontologies with our APIs.

Content Scraper

Scrape RAW data you need from anywhere in the world

NLU Engine

Identify and extract exactly what you need from a simple RAW text.

Playground

Ontology Parser

Have any dataset converted into an efficient heirachial Ontology

Playground

Ontology Merger

Merge any 2 Ontologies together efficiently.

Who is AKURA Platform for?

For Developers

Focus on the important parts of your application without having to worry about how to get the data you need.

For Researchers

Focus on the knowledge graph ontology provided by us without having to worry about how to convert your existing dataset to a knowledge graph.

For Enterprises

Focus on the meaningful data extracted by the NLU engine and Content Scraper without having to find efficient ways extract data manually.

For Rapid Development

Save time in your development and use the tools and apis provided by AKURA to quickly develop your application the way you want it to be.
